Fitness and Impact

I belive impact happens not only in research paper and publications, it also happens in day to day activities and with the everyday people. Hence I would love to give this local run club a shout-out. Run for Resilience is a run club founded by Canberrans, aims at showcasing the world a suicide free ACT by 2033. They are on a mission to connect our community with support, movement, and meaning.
