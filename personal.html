If you are in Canberra, check out....

Cafe/Breakfast:
Mocan & Green Grout, their baked eggs are amazing!
Cupping Room, suprisingly good matcha.
Two before Ten (Aranda) , satified my avocado toast + cold brew craving all the time.

Whiskey/Cocktail Bar:

Molly's, their whiskey apple have a special place in my heart.
Volstead Repeal, jazzy & vintage atmosphere, great scotch selection.

Beer bar:
Bent Spoke, iconic, try their "Crankshaft" IPA!
Capital Brewing Co, their beer after boudering at Bloc is always a good idea.

Wine bar:
Dear Prudence, the first place that I tried "orange wine", lovely ambiance.
Margot Wine & Espresso Bar, frozen rose after a run by the lake always hits differently.

Restaurant:
Savoir Francais (previously La Baugette). "The" place to get baugette in Canberra.
Pizza Artigiana, my new favorite pizzaria.
Terra, great for lunch, the best value around city, I reckon.
